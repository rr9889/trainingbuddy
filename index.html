2️⃣ Evidence‑Based Principles

  • Frequency ≥ 2×/week per muscle beats traditional bro‑splits.
  • 10‑20 hard sets/week captures ≈ 90 % of growth; returns diminish past that.
  • Effort close to failure (1–2 RIR) even with light DBs still maximises hypertrophy.
  • Protein 1.6–2.2 g·kg⁻¹·day⁻¹ proven to support lean‑mass gains.
  • Progressive overload: add reps → add load → repeat.

4️⃣ Load & Progression Rules

  • Work to 1–2 RIR (~RPE 8‑9) on last set.
  • When you reach the top of the rep range for every set two workouts straight, add 2.5‑5 lb per DB next session.
  • If reps fall below range, drop 5 lb.
  • Deload every 6‑8 weeks: halve set count for one week.

5️⃣ Nutrition & Recovery Checklist

  • Caloric surplus ≈ +250 kcal/day (aim 0.25‑0.5 kg gain / month).
  • Protein 1.6‑2.2 g·kg⁻¹ ·day⁻¹, spread across ≥4 meals.
  • Creatine 3‑5 g daily.
  • Sleep 7‑9 h; deep sleep drives recovery hormones.

6️⃣ Quick FAQ

QuestionAnswer
How do I know the load is right?Last 2 reps slow but smooth; form stays clean.
Does warm‑up count toward volume?No. Perform 1‑2 lighter sets before first big move.
What if 55 lb becomes easy?Add tempo (3‑sec ecc.), pauses, or extend rep range to 15.
How fast do I add weight?Bump load once all sets hit top reps at ≤2 RIR.
